What are cookies?

Cookies are tiny text files stored on your device when you access a website, and they perform a wide variety of tasks. On spinmacho, they are primarily used to remember user preferences, maintain session stability, and deliver tailored features. These files are not harmful to your device and cannot spread viruses. However, they hold identifiers that help recognize repeat visits, making the overall navigation smoother. The role of cookies on spin macho includes both functional support and analytics tracking. They play a crucial role in aligning the site’s performance with user expectations.
